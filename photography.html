PHOTOGRAPHY

For me, photography is more than capturing an image, it's about capturing a feeling. Each frame is a way of slowing down time, of finding joy in the small details and beauty in the everyday. When the world feels uncertain, I turn to my camera to add a little light, a sparkle of laughter, a hint of wonder, and a reminder that life is still full of magic.
